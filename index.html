About VitalBloom Project

Project Overview

This project aims to develop a comprehensive mobile application to address diabetes and obesity, chronic diseases affecting millions globally. VitalBloom will integrate advanced AI algorithms to non-invasively estimate body fat and glucose levels using user data and smartphone sensors. It will also provide personalized meal plans (based on a local food database) and exercise/yoga routines tailored to individual goals, including a robust tracking system with notifications and gamification to improve adherence. Designed to operate without external devices, it prioritizes accessibility in vulnerable areas, where specialized care is often lacking, to reduce healthcare costs, inequalities, and complications linked to these diseases, aligning with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 3).

The Challenge: Problem Statement

Diabetes and obesity represent interconnected global public health crises, affecting millions and driving significant healthcare costs. In Colombia, a high prevalence of overweight, obesity, and diabetes, exacerbated by unhealthy lifestyles and limited access to preventive care, creates an urgent demand for accessible, personalized health tools. These conditions severely impact life expectancy and account for a large portion of global deaths, deepening health inequalities. Our project aims to provide a viable solution through a mobile application integrating non-invasive metabolic estimation and tailored health plans, reducing economic and educational barriers and contributing to sustainable development goals.

Our Core Inquiry: Research Question

"How to develop a mobile application, with metabolic estimation and personalized habit management, to contribute to the prevention and comprehensive management of diabetes and obesity?"

Our Guiding Principle: Hypothesis

The integrated mobile application for people with diabetes and obesity will keep track of their glucose levels, their body mass index and their weight in kilograms, and will improve people's quality of life, through nutritional plans based on artificial intelligence.

Why This Matters: Justification

This research project is fundamentally important due to the pervasive global health crisis posed by diabetes and obesity, conditions that affect millions and place immense strain on healthcare systems worldwide. In Colombia, the substantial prevalence of these conditions, aggravated by lifestyle choices and inadequate access to preventive care in rural areas, underscores the critical need for effective interventions. These diseases not only diminish life expectancy but also significantly contribute to global mortality rates and exacerbate health inequalities, making urgent action essential for sustainable public health.

Our proposed mobile application offers a powerful solution by eliminating common economic and educational barriers to health management. By providing non-invasive metabolic estimations, localized nutritional guidance, and tailored exercise routines, it empowers individuals to proactively manage their health. The integrated tracking, notification, and gamification features are designed to enhance adherence, ultimately improving users' quality of life. This low-cost, highly accessible technological intervention aims to mitigate health disparities and prevent complications, directly aligning with global health objectives (SDG 3). Without such innovations, the burden of these diseases would continue to escalate, leading to further health decline and unsustainable pressure on healthcare infrastructures.

Project Goals: Objectives

General Objective

  • To Develop a mobile application that, through metabolic estimation and personalized habit management, contributes to the prevention and comprehensive management of diabetes and obesity.

Specific Objectives

  • To research diabetes, obesity, and current methods for managing these conditions (e.g., exercise, diets, medications).
  • To learn to develop mobile applications using the Python programming language.
  • To design an algorithm integrating the above objectives to create a nutritional plan that includes exercise, diet, and habits to improve the quality of life for individuals with these conditions.
  • To conduct pilot tests to evaluate the effectiveness and efficiency of the nutritional plans.

Future Outlook: What's Next?

VitalBloom is constantly evolving. Our vision extends to continuous improvement, including:

  • Advanced AI Models: Incorporating more sophisticated machine learning algorithms for even more accurate metabolic estimations and personalized recommendations.
  • Expanded Content: Adding a wider variety of local food databases, exercise routines, and mindfulness practices.
  • Community Features: Implementing social features to foster user interaction, support groups, and shared challenges.
  • Integration with Wearables: Exploring seamless integration with smartwatches and other health monitoring devices for real-time data synchronization.
  • Accessibility Enhancements: Further improving the app's accessibility for users with diverse needs, including multilingual support.
We are committed to making VitalBloom a leading tool in preventive health and chronic disease management.
